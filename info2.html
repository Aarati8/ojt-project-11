Visitor Parking (School/Campus Information)

Parking Information

Due to the limited parking availability around school/campus, we strongly recommend that visitors consider using public transportation for their convenience and to avoid parking difficulties.

Key Landmarks for Bus Routes:

  • Maharajgunj Chowk / Maharajgunj Bus Stop
  • Teaching Hospital (TUTH)
  • Office of the President (Rastrapati Bhawan)
  • Budhanilkantha (many buses to Maharajgunj continue to Budhanilkantha)

Bus Routes to Shivapuri Secondary School

Shivapuri Secondary School is located in Maharajgunj, Kathmandu, close to the Tribhuvan University Teaching Hospital (TUTH) and the Office of the President.

Key Landmarks for Bus Routes (Tell these to conductors/drivers):

  • Maharajgunj Chowk / Maharajgunj Bus Stop
  • Teaching Hospital (TUTH)
  • Office of the President (Rastrapati Bhawan)
  • Budhanilkantha (many buses to Maharajgunj continue to Budhanilkantha)

Bus Operators & Routes:

Sajha Yatayat Buses (Known for larger, more comfortable buses):
  • From Ratna Park (City Center): Look for Sajha buses heading towards Budhanilkantha. These buses will pass through Maharajgunj.
  • From Jamal (Central Kathmandu): Take the Sajha bus route that terminates at Teaching Hospital. This is a very direct option.
  • From Lagankhel (Lalitpur): Sajha has routes (e.g., Route 1) that go to Gongabu New Bus Park via Maharajgunj (Teaching Hospital, Narayan Gopal Chowk).
  • From other parts of Lalitpur (e.g., Pulchowk, Jawalakhel, Kupondole): Many Sajha routes connect these areas to Maharajgunj, often terminating at New Bus Park or Budhanilkantha.
Local Private Buses / Micro Buses (Smaller, more frequent):
  • From major hubs (e.g., Ratna Park, Old Bus Park, Kalanki, Koteshwor, Gongabu Bus Park, Lagankhel):
    • Look for buses and micro-buses with destination signs reading:
