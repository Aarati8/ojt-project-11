Athletics and Recreation

At Shivapuri Secondary School, physical activity is considered an essential part of student development. We strive to maintain an environment where students not only focus on academics but also actively participate in sports and recreational programs. These activities help build discipline, teamwork, and confidence among students.

School Playground

Our open playground is a lively hub of activity during school hours. It serves as a multipurpose space for games, morning assemblies, PE classes, and casual breaks. Students enjoy playing football, volleyball, badminton, and other traditional games such as karate and chungi. Though modest in size, the playground is used daily and brings great energy to school life.

Annual Sports Week

One of the most exciting events at our school is the Annual Sports Week. Students from different grades and from different house form teams to compete in various athletic competitions like volleyball, baseketball, football, badminton and cricket. The event promotes healthy competition and unity, and medals and certificates are awarded to winners, making it a cherished memory for all.

Recreational Activities

Besides sports, students are encouraged to join recreational sessions such as group dancing, PT, and singing during club periods or before school events. These creative outlets provide balance and reduce stress, especially during exam seasons. Occasional movie screenings or storytelling sessions also help students unwind and have fun.

Team Representation

Selected students have the opportunity to represent Shivapuri in inter-school sports tournaments organized by local municipalities and education offices. These competitions help our students interact with peers from other schools and boost their confidence and sportsmanship.

Health and Wellness

The health and safety of our students is a top priority at Shivapuri Secondary School. While we may not have a full hospital on campus/school, we maintain essential health services and promote wellness through education and community support.

School Health Room

Our school maintains a simple but effective nurse room equipped with basic first-aid kits and supplies. Minor injuries, sudden illnesses, and health complaints are handled immediately by trained staff. Students who feel unwell during school hours are given initial care, and if necessary, parents are informed and appropriate steps are taken.

Emergency Response

In case of medical emergencies, our teachers and administrative staff are trained to respond quickly and responsibly. We have coordination with the nearest health post, and arrangements are made to transport students if needed. Emergency contact information for every student is safely kept and used when necessary.

Health Education

As part of our curriculum, we provide lessons on personal hygiene, nutrition, mental health, and adolescent development. Special sessions are conducted by visiting health professionals to educate students about common illnesses, menstruation hygiene, and healthy habits. These sessions are especially important for growing students in rural and semi-urban areas.

Awareness Programs

From time to time, health screening programs such as eye-checkups and dental checkups are organized in collaboration with local health volunteers and NGOs. The school also observes important dates like World Health Day with rallies, poster-making, and student-led presentations to spread awareness on health-related topics.

Arts and Culture

Honoring Culture Through Rituals

Students and teachers participating in a traditional Newar cultural puja held within the school premises. This ritual, rooted in centuries-old heritage, promotes spiritual mindfulness and respect for ancestral customs. Such events give students the opportunity to engage with Nepal's rich cultural legacy, building awareness and appreciation of local traditions.

Honoring Our Educators

During our Annual Day celebration, dedicated teachers were honored with certificates in recognition of their tireless efforts and commitment to quality education. This reflects our deep appreciation for their contributions in shaping students' futures.

Student Art Exhibition

Our talented students showcase their creativity through beautiful collections of drawings and paintings. The artwork captures themes like nature, culture, and imagination, demonstrating deep observation and expression skills.

Cultural Performances

Students perform traditional Nepali dances and cultural shows during our Annual Day celebrations. These performances celebrate our rich cultural heritage and build students' confidence from an early age.

Student Activities

Shivapuri Secondary School offers a vibrant mix of co-curricular activities that allow students to explore their passions and grow as active members of society. These activities provide a balance to academics and encourage students to be expressive, responsible, and creative.

Cultural Celebrations

We celebrate events such as Children’s Day, Constitution Day, Saraswati Puja, National Democracy Day, and other special occasions. Students actively participate in dance, drama, music, poetry, and storytelling performances that showcase our rich culture and student talent. These events are filled with joy and enthusiasm, uniting the school community.

Competitions and Challenges

The school regularly organizes essay writing, art and drawing, quiz contests, speech competitions, spelling bees, and math challenges. These activities help students develop confidence, communication skills, and critical thinking. Inter-house and inter-grade competitions bring a fun and competitive spirit to the school year.

Student Clubs

  • STEAM Club: Explores Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics through experiments and innovative projects.
  • BABA Club: Focuses on environmental cleanliness, tree planting, and maintaining school greenery.
  • Eco Club: Works on sustainability, recycling, and environmental conservation education.
  • Canopy Nepal Programs: Provides creative workshops, life skills training, and digital education sessions.

Community Engagement

Students participate in school sanitation drives, anti-drug awareness rallies, and health camps, helping them become responsible and socially aware citizens.
