Overview

Core SEE Preparation
The curriculum prioritizes Nepali (essay writing on societal issues), English (formal letter drafting), and Mathematics (SEE-aligned algebra/trigonometry). Science combines theory (chemical equations) with mandatory labs (titration).

Social Studies & Electives
Students analyze Nepal's federal structure and economic systems. Electives like Computer Basics teach Word/Excel skills, while Accountancy introduces ledger maintenance for small businesses.

Practical Learning
Science fairs (hydroponic farming models)
Community surveys (local waste management practices)
Debate competitions on national issues (federalism pros/cons)

Holistic Development
Physical education includes traditional games (dandi biyo), and arts programs feature madal drumming workshops. Life skills sessions cover financial literacy (calculating compound interest).

Post-SEE Pathways
Options include:
Grade 11 Science/Management streams
Vocational training (tourism/hospitality)
Direct entry into administrative roles

Academic Tracks

For classes 9 and 10, the Curriculum Development Centre (CDC) in Nepal offers both academic and technical/vocational streams. The academic stream includes subjects like Nepali, English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and optional subjects like Computer Science, Economics, and more. The technical/vocational stream offers courses like Animal Science, Civil Engineering, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Plants Science.

Cirriculum

The non-technical stream prioritizes holistic development through Nepal’s national curriculum. Science labs (e.g., titration experiments) and debate clubs enhance critical thinking, while electives like Computer Basics introduce digital literacy. Competencies include essay writing, algebraic problem-solving, and explaining scientific phenomena—culminating in the SEE exams

Based on the above context, the following competencies have been determined for early childhood development and education:

(A) Competence

Core Subjects

  • Solve quadratic equations (SEE paper pattern).
  • Write formal letters in English/Nepali with proper structure.
  • Demonstrate titration techniques in chemistry lab.

Social Sciences

  • Map Nepal's provinces with key river systems.
  • Analyze causes of 2007 Madhesh movement.
  • Prepare basic financial statements (Trial Balance).

Life Skills

  • Deliver 5-minute speeches on current issues.
  • Calculate compound interest for loan scenarios.
  • Create PowerPoint presentations with animations.
