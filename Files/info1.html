The Basics (Visitor Information)

Where is Shivapuri Secondary School?

"Situated adjacent to the Office of the President of Nepal, Shivapuri Secondary School is one of the top educational centers of Kathmandu Metropolitan City. It is based in the north part of Kathmandu, i.e., Maharajgunj. Shivapuri Secondary School is part of an area highly popular for having a high density of educational centers, including government schools with high profile serving both academic and technical streams. Shivapuri Secondary School remains part of this vibrant educational hub, providing quality education and contributing to the development of wholesome individuals."

"As one of Nepal's superior schools, Shivapuri Secondary School is deeply rooted in its society and nation, rich in diversity of experience. The school is a living heritage of Nepal's commitment to education, where it is committed to excellence in scholarship and development in general. In this vibrant learning atmosphere, Shivapuri Secondary School adds even more to the experience as a community center. It houses a variety of extracurricular activities, community functions, and programs that combine study in academics with grass root involvement, and therefore, it forms a vital part as part of Nepal's education and social scene."

"Learn about Shivapuri Secondary School, a beacon of Kathmandu education. As a future student, a parent looking for quality education, or just an inquiring member of our community, this welcome provides a comprehensive look at what sets our school apart and makes it so important. Learn about our focus on academic achievement, wholesome growth, and community outreach as we prepare Nepal's future leaders."

Individual Visit

We are contented to welcome you to explore our school/campus and experience the vital sprit of School hands-on. As you prepare for your visit, we aspire to provide you with all the necessary information to ensure your experience be both enriching and seamless.

As a dedicated place for teaching, Schivapuri Secondary School warmly opens its doors to visitors without charging any fees. We kindly remind all the visitors to strictly follow the legal regulations and school's rules, contributing to the maintenance of a harmonious school/campus environment.

To visit our school/campus, please use our reservation system. Currently, all visits require real-name registration through the "Shivapuri Secondary School" Messanger/Facebook applet(Nepali). Please note that due to some system upgrade, the applet currently supports only identify verification using school id card. If you are visiting from Japan, South Korea, China, India, USA and Canada, please feel free to contact us at 01-4410877 and 01-4422877(available from 7:00 to 16:00) 1-7 days prior to your planned visit to arrange your reservation. We ask all visitors to enter from school/campus gate as specified in your confirmation

Visitor Registration

The "Visit Shivapuri" mini program currently only supports school ID card verification. Visitors from Local municipality, Office of Prime Minister and foreign visitors like: Japan, South Korea, China, India, USA and Canada are encouraged to initiate their visit arrangements by sending an email to shivapuri@enet.com.np, 1-7 days in advance. The email should indicate the name of the person making the reservation, the type and number of the certificate, the contact information, the date and time slot of the appointment, and a scanned copy or photo of the certificate. The system will automatically process the application, and successful reservations will be notified via email.
