About

Shivapuri Secondary School is a public institution known for its commitment to quality education and holistic development.

General Information

Shivapuri Secondary School and College, established in 2005 in Kathmandu, is a public institution affiliated with NEB and TU.

History

Founded in 2005, Shivapuri Secondary School aimed to provide inclusive, quality education and became a key player in Nepal's education sector.





Current Administration

Shivapuri School is run by a committed faculty and management team, working with local authorities to improve education.

Formal Presidents

Shivapuri Secondary School has been guided by several visionary leaders whose contributions helped shape its values, infrastructure, and progressive educational environment.
