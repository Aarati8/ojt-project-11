SHIVAPURI AT INTERNATIONAL LEVEL

CANOPY NEPAL

Canopy Nepal, an educational non-profit, partnered with Shivapuri Secondary School to support inclusive and interactive learning. Through this collaboration, students participated in workshops focused on language, leadership, and cultural awareness. The initiative helped enhance students' confidence and communication skills. It also encouraged critical thinking and active participation in their education journey.

INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL AWARDS

Shivapuri Secondary School proudly received the prestigious International School Award (ISA), recognizing its commitment to global education and international collaboration. Through various cross-cultural projects and partnerships, students engaged with global issues and built connections beyond the classroom. The ISA program enhanced the school's curriculum, encouraging creativity, communication, and critical thinking. This achievement highlights our school’s dedication to preparing students for a global future.
