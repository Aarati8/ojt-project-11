Scholarships:

Admissions:

  • Admission for Nursery to Grade 9 usually begins in the first or second week of Baisakh (Nepali month) each academic year.
  • Application forms for Grade XI are available immediately after the SEE results are published.
  • Students who have achieved at least a C grade are eligible to apply for Grade XI.

Academic Programs:

The school offers a wide range of programs, including classes from Primary to Grade 12, a Ten Plus Two (+2) Management program, and a Computer Engineering technical program for grades 9-12.
