Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS)

Our Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS) program is a four-year degree designed to provide students with comprehensive knowledge of business principles and practices. The program prepares graduates for leadership roles in various business sectors or for further academic pursuits.

Program Highlights

Duration

4 years

Credit Hours

120 credit hours

Class Schedule

Morning (6:30 AM - 10:00 AM)

Degree Awarded

Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS)

Program Objectives

  • Develop fundamental knowledge of business and management principles
  • Enhance analytical and decision-making skills
  • Prepare students for professional careers in business and government
  • Develop entrepreneurial skills for self-employment
  • Provide foundation for postgraduate studies in business

Career Prospects

Graduates of our BBS program find opportunities in various sectors including:

  • Banking and Financial Institutions
  • Corporate Sector
  • Government Organizations
  • Non-governmental Organizations
  • Entrepreneurship and Business Ownership
  • Further Academic Studies (MBA, MBS, etc.)

BBS Curriculum

The BBS program follows a structured curriculum designed to provide comprehensive business education. The program consists of foundation courses, core business courses, specialization courses, and a research project in the final year.

Course Structure

Year Focus Areas Credit Hours
First Year Business Fundamentals, Economics, Accounting 30
Second Year Business Law, Finance, Marketing, Organizational Behavior 30
Third Year Business Research, Taxation, Banking, Human Resource 30
Fourth Year Strategic Management, Entrepreneurship, Research Project 30

Core Courses

  • Principles of Management
  • Business Economics
  • Financial Accounting
  • Business Mathematics
  • Business Communication
  • Marketing Management
  • Organizational Behavior
  • Business Law
  • Financial Management
  • Strategic Management

Admission Requirements

Admission to our BBS program is competitive and based on academic qualifications. Below are the requirements for the program:

Eligibility Criteria

  • Completion of higher secondary education (10+2 or equivalent) in any discipline
  • Minimum GPA of 2.0 or second division marks in higher secondary
  • Passing score in the entrance examination conducted by the college
  • Proficiency in English (written and spoken)

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Attested copies of academic transcripts (SEE and +2)
  • Character certificate from last institution attended
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs
  • Migration certificate (for students from other boards)

Admission Process

  1. Submit completed application form with required documents
  2. Appear for entrance examination (if applicable)
  3. Attend interview (if shortlisted)
  4. Pay admission fee upon selection
  5. Complete registration process

Important Dates

Process Date
