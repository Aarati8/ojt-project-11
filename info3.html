Shivapuri Secondary School: Comprehensive Infrastructure Walkthrough

1. Academic Infrastructure

a. Classroom Complexes

  • Primary Wing (Grades 1-5)
    • 15 brightly lit, child-friendly classrooms with ergonomic furniture.
    • Walls adorned with educational posters (Nepali alphabet, multiplication tables).
    • Interactive corners (e.g., "Nature Table" for science displays).
  • Secondary Wing (Grades 6-10)
    • 20 classrooms equipped with smart boards and projectors for digital lessons.
    • SEE preparation rooms with past exam papers and reference charts.
  • Higher Secondary Wing (Grades 11-12)
    • Stream-specific classrooms:
      • Science: Periodic tables, anatomical models.
      • Management: Whiteboards with accounting equation templates.
      • Computer Engineering: Dual-monitor coding stations.

b. Laboratories

  • Physics Lab
    • Equipment: Vernier calipers, circuit kits, prism sets for optics experiments.
    • Safety: Fire extinguishers, first-aid kits, and grounded electrical outlets.
  • Chemistry Lab
    • Fume hoods, reagent racks (acids/bases labeled per safety standards).
    • Student workstations with sinks and Bunsen burners.
  • Biology Lab
    • Microscopes (100x‚Äì400x), specimen jars (frog dissection kits).
    • Herbarium collections of local flora (e.g., Rhododendron arboreum).
  • Computer Labs
    • Lab A (Grades 9-10): 30 PCs with C/C++ compilers, LAN setup for networking practice.
    • Lab B (Grades 11-12): High-end machines for Python/Java, 3D modeling software (Blender).

2. Administrative and Support Facilities

a. Library & Resource Center

  • Main Library
    • 5,000+ books (Nepali/English), including CDC/NEB-approved textbooks.
    • Digital section with e-learning terminals (Khan Academy access).
  • Reading Garden
    • Outdoor seating under Champa trees for leisure reading.

b. Health and Wellness

  • Medical Room
    • Staffed by a trained nurse; stocks basic medicines and AED devices.
    • Monthly health camps (vision/dental check-ups).
  • Counseling Center
    • Private sessions for career guidance (NEB/SEE counseling) and mental health.

c. Cafeteria and Hygiene

  • Dining Hall
    • Capacity: 150 students; serves dal-bhat, snacks, and hygienic packaged drinks.
    • Solar-powered water filters for safe drinking water.
  • Sanitation
    • Separate toilets for staff/students (Western and squat styles).
    • Biodegradable waste composting pit.

3. Extracurricular and Recreational Spaces

a. Sports Complex

  • Outdoor
    • Basketball/volleyball courts with floodlights for evening practice.
    • Cricket nets and football field (maintained for inter-school matches).
  • Indoor
    • Table tennis, chess, and carrom boards in the auditorium.

b. Arts and Culture

  • Music Room
    • Traditional instruments (madal, sarangi), piano, and sound system.
  • Art Studio
    • Pottery wheels, acrylic/oil paints, and exhibitions of student work.

c. Auditorium

  • 300-seat capacity for assemblies, debates, and cultural programs (e.g., Deusi-Bhailo during Tihar).

4. Specialized Infrastructure

a. Technical Stream Facilities

  • Engineering Workshop
    • Tools for hardware projects (e.g., Raspberry Pi kits, soldering stations).
  • Maker Space
    • 3D printer, robotics kits (Arduino/LEGO Mindstorms).

b. Green Initiatives

  • School surrounded by greeen plants and beautiful flowers.
  • School Gardening:
    • Students grow plants and flowers as the team member of BABA club.

5. Safety and Accessibility

  • Security
    • CCTV cameras at entry/exit points, guarded by G4S personnel.
  • Disaster Preparedness
    • Earthquake drills quarterly; emergency exits marked with glow paint.
  • Accessibility
    • Ramps and tactile paths for visually impaired students.
